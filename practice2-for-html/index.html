Souffle

It is quite practical if you read the tricks carefully, to prepare the soufflé, which is one of the shortest time to cook chocolate desserts, and which is a visual feast thanks to its liquid interior and that you will want to eat as you eat. Thus, you will be able to make that wonderful soufflé you always go out and eat at a cafe at home and serve it with your recipes.

Ingredients for Chocolate Souffle Recipe

  • 125 gram dark chocolate
  • 125 milliliter milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 spoonful butter
  • 1.5 tablespoons of flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar

How to Make Chocolate Souffle Recipe?

  1. Take 125 milliliters of milk in a saucepan and heat until it reaches the boiling point.
  2. Add the chocolate into it. Once the chocolate has melted, take it off the heat.
  3. In a separate saucepan, melt 2 tablespoons of butter.
  4. Add 1.5 tablespoons of flour on it and fry it until it smells.
  5. After the smell of the flour is gone, add it into the chocolate milk mixture and add 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar and mix it continuously.
  6. Add 3 egg yolks, mix for 2 more minutes, then remove from the stove and leave to cool.
  7. Whisk 3 egg whites in a deep bowl with a pinch of salt until they do not flow when you turn the bowl upside down.
  8. Combine the cooled chocolate mixture with the foamy egg whites and mix with the help of a spatula.
  9. Divide the mortar into greased and cocoa-coated soufflé cups, leaving a finger gap, and bake in a preheated 200 degree oven for 10 minutes.
  10. As soon as it comes out of the oven, sprinkle powdered sugar on it and serve, bon appetit!
